Mexican Alphabet Soup

  • In a blender add: 1-4 roma tomatoes, 1 piece of garlic, 1 tbsp caldo de sabor de pollo, 4 cups of water.
  • In a pan heat up oil
  • Add Alphabet Macaroni Pasta and toast until golden brown
  • When pasta is toasty add the blended mixture
  • Keep on med heat for 8 minutes or until it starts to boil
  • Turn heat off and keep lid on
  • Let cool off and enjoy :D
